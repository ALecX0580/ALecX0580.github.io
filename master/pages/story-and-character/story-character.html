Story Summary

Step back 100 years before the epic events of Homeworld to the harsh, uncharted deserts of Kharak. Deserts of Kharak follows a daring military expedition led by Rachel Sjet, an ancestor of the legendary Karan Sjet. On a mission to uncover a mysterious anomaly detected by an orbital satellite.

Set around 1110 KDS, this thrilling prequel explores the discovery of the enigmatic Khar-Toba, a powerful artifact buried deep within the Great Banded Desert.
