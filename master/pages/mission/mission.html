Missions

These missions are from the campaign you will encounter.

Mission One

Rachel S'jet arrives at Epsilon Base, where the S'jet Command Carrier is preparing for a deep desert expedition. The mission focuses on establishing a foothold in the harsh desert environment, setting the stage for the journey ahead.

Mission Two

The Kapisi arrives at a S'jet salvage facility amidst escalating Gaalsien attacks across the Northern Federation. This mission involves conducting last-minute upgrades to prepare for the challenges of the open desert.

Mission Three

Departing from the Boneyard, the Kapisi ventures into the open desert, aiming to rendezvous with another Coalition Carrier, the Sakala of Kiith Siidim. The mission emphasizes navigating the vast and treacherous terrain.

Mission Fourth

Following the discovery of the Ifriit Naabal and Jacob S'jet's log, the Kapisi heads towards the Sarathi Basin in search of the enigmatic Kalash Site. The mission centers on exploration and uncovering hidden secrets beneath the sands.

Mission Fifth

With the threat of a Gaalsien attack looming, Rachel and her team devise a plan to initiate shipbreaking, resourcing, and artifact recovery operations at the Kalash Wreck site. The mission focuses on resource management and strategic planning.

Mission Sixth

After damaging the Gaalsien Carrier Ashoka, the Sakala and Kapisi pursue their enemy into the Baladin Dune Sea, aiming to finish off the threat. The mission involves high-stakes combat in a vast and unforgiving desert landscape.

Mission Seventh

Having destroyed the Ashoka, the Kapisi and Sakala go on the offensive to attack a Gaalsien Base. The mission focuses on tactical assaults and strategic objectives to weaken the enemy's hold.

Mission Eight

With the Gaalsien military base destroyed, the Kapisi pushes forward towards their ultimate goal: the Primary Anomaly. The mission delves into ancient ruins, uncovering long-forgotten mysteries.

Mission Ninth

Having overcome Gaalsien opposition and collected valuable artifacts, the Kapisi sets course for the Whispering Gallery, a gateway to the Dreamlands. The mission explores the intersection of technology and ancient knowledge.

Mission Tenth

With their resupply from Tiir inbound, the Kapisi and Sakala must secure the Khashar Plateau as a landing site, despite heavy Gaalsien resistance. The mission emphasizes defensive strategies and coordination with allied forces.

Mission Eleventh

Having secured the plateau, the Kapisi and her crew must now hold it against fierce Gaalsien counterattacks. The mission focuses on fortifying positions and repelling enemy offensives.

Mission Twelfth

Betrayed by their allies, the Siidim, the Kapisi and her crew race to the Torin Crater to save Rachel. The mission involves navigating treacherous terrain and making critical decisions under pressure.

Mission Thirteenth

With the Siidim eliminated and the Gaalsien held at bay by the Orbital Weapon, the Kapisi and Rachel finally reach their ultimate goal: the Primary Anomaly itself. The mission culminates in a climactic confrontation, determining the fate of Kharak.
