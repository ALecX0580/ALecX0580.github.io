The dream began long ago, a whisper carried on the wind
A voice from the depths of the desert, calling to us.
My brother Jacob believed he could hear it—the Guidestone
An ancient artifact lost beneath the sands of Kharak.
For years, we searched, driven by a belief that it held the key to our future
and when we finally unearthed it, everything changed.
The Northern Coalition saw our discovery as a threat
accusing us of violating the fragile ceasefire, and the desert erupted into war.
They came for us with their fleets, determined to stop what they didn’t understand.
Now, the world is dying, the very sand poisoned by the escalating conflict
And the only hope left for our people lies far to the north—deep in hostile territory—where the Jaraci Object waits.
We must reach it.
Failure is not an option.
I am Rachel S’jet, science officer of the Kapisi expedition
And at dawn, we leave to face the desert and whatever else awaits us beyond the horizon.
